Singapore Business Federation’s Young Business Leaders Network (SBF-YBLN) HOPE Fund receives a S$50 million funding partnership boost with CIMB Bank Singapore and Goldbell Financial Services

The SBF-YBLN announces a S$50 million funding partnership with CIMB Bank Singapore and Goldbell Financial Services GBFS in support of the SBF-YBLN HOPE Fund that will bring further financial support to SMEs impacted by the COVID-19 pandemic.

Resilience Budget addressed businesses’ most immediate concerns, a recent SBF poll reveals

The poll revealed that Singapore businesses have largely acknowledged that the Resilience Budget has been timely and impactful, with measures that have helped them meet their immediate needs, especially in the areas of wage costs and cashflow issues.
